About FilterGif

FilterGif lets you make animated GIF slideshows using your Instagram photos and some imagination

I could say that FilterGif allows you to create animated images that employ Soviet montage theory in which short shots are edited into a sequence to condense space, time, and information. Instead I'll say that FilterGif is a fun little project I created that lets you make cool image sequences which you can share with your friends. Or you can keep them to yourself, I'm not going to tell you what to do.

Created Using:

  • Python
  • Flask
  • Foundation
  • Instagram's API
